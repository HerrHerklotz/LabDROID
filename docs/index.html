LabDroid

Androidifyed Prototyping

Your benefits

Don't waste your money. Your phone includes a huge set of high end components which are just waiting to be used.

Invest your time into your project and not the setup. LabDroid gives your direct access to all phone components.

Avoid additional environment damage. You do not have to order boards from Chin if your phone is right next to you.

Graphical programming

Realizing your idea has never been easier than with LabDroid. Just pick & place your phone components as nodes on your workspace and connect the ports to enable a highspeed dataflow between them.

JavaScript supercharged

Through an embedded JavaScript interpreter and a special script node you are able to develop algorithems direct on your device.

Remote development

Because your phone screen is to small to do serious programming on it, LabDroid comes with an HTML based IDE.
