LabDroid

Welcome to the first all including rapid prototyping environment for Android.

Realize your next tech project with ease

LabDroid gives your the opportunity to implement a wide range of functions in an faster way like never before.

How do it works?

Extend your project with a new feature (e.g. GPS) requires three main tasks to be done:

  • Find (and buy $$$) a matching chip
  • Read and understand the manual
  • Implement a bunch of functions to get this thing running
LabDroid jumps in place and delivers you direct access to the functions and hardware of your Android device with an easy to use graphical programming interface. This saves your lifetime, energy and money.
